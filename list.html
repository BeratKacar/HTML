Simple Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup floor
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • butter for cooking
    • Or oil

Ordered List

Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the egg and milk, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little butter or oil.
  4. Pour some batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  5. Flip the pancake and cook for another minute until golden brown.
  6. Serve with honey, syrup, or fruit.

Pancakes!!
ancakes are a quick and easy breakfast dish made from a simple batter. To prepare them, first, mix the dry ingredients—flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt—in a bowl. Then, add the egg and milk, stirring until the batter becomes smooth and free of lumps. Next, heat a pan over medium heat and add a little butter or oil to prevent sticking. Pour some batter into the pan, forming a round shape. When bubbles start to appear on the surface, flip the pancake and cook the other side until it turns golden brown. Once cooked, serve the pancakes with honey, syrup, or fresh fruit for extra flavor. Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
